Our Ethos and Vision

We work with others to create a healthy, safe environment within which all our students can enjoy learning, make progress and achieve high standards. We expect all students to participate fully and make a positive contribution to the school and wider community; and, ultimately become successful, motivated, well-adjusted adults who achieve economic well-being.

Our History

Rishton Academy Primary School is one of the top schools in the country and has enjoyed 100 years of success.Rishton Academy School was founded by Miss Emily Singleton on April 19th, 1923. Her creation of a school which now has more than 700 pupils, superb facilities and is well known for unrivalled holistic excellence is remarkable. Just becoming qualified was a feat for a woman in the 1920s.
